Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Creamy, delicious, and ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta (or bacon)
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt & Black Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, fry pancetta with olive oil and garlic until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with grated Parmesan and black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta (reserve some pasta water), then add to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat, stir in egg mixture quickly to avoid scrambling.
  6. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  7. Serve hot, topped with extra Parmesan and pepper.
